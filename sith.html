General Info About Siths

Siths hold lightsabers, typically red color. They are more powerful than Jedi because Siths are taught the dark side of the force, and the Jedi are taught the typically weaker light side. The dark side of the force involves choking people without making direct contact, shooting lightning from your fingers, and many more abilities that Jedi do not possess. Siths are also typically more skilled at lightsaber dueling.


What Are Lightsabers?

In the movies, lightsabers are created with crystals found in the planet Ilum. These crystals are used to power the lightsabers. The energy from the crystal is shot through the lightsaber focusing energy channel, which creates the laser blade. This energy channel is opened with the push of a button, which activates the laser blade.
