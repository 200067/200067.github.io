Brief Summary of Each Movie

Star Wars Episode I, the Phantom Menace

Obi-Wan Kenobi is a young Jedi knight under the training of Qui-Gon Jinn, his master. Anakin Skywalker, who will later give birth to Luke Skywalker and become known as Darth Vader, is currently a 9-year-old boy. When the Trade Federation closes all paths to the planet Naboo, Qui-Gon and Obi-Wan are sent to conclude the issue.

Star Wars Episode II, the Attack of the Clones

Ten years after the crisis of the Phantom Menace the Republic continues to be surrounded with chaos. A movement involving hundreds of planets and huge alliances creates new threats to the galaxy that even the Republic and the Jedis cannot maintain. This movement, long planned by an unrevealed force, resulted in the beginning of the Clone Wars, and the start of a massive threat to the Republic.

Star Wars Episode III, the Revenge of the Sith

Three years after the start of the Clone Wars, Jedi Master Obi-Wan Kenobi and Jedi Knight Anakin Skywalker work together to save Chancellor Palpatine from Grievous, the leader of the droid army, but Grievous escapes immediately afterwards. Accusations are created within the Jedi Council involving Chancellor Palpatine, to which Anakin has befriended. After Anakin is asked to spy on the chancellor, and full of hatred toward the Jedi Council, Anakin joins the Dark Side.

Star Wars Episode IV, A New Hope

The Imperial Forces, under orders of Darth Vader, who used to be Anakin Skywalker but turned to the dark side, hold Princess Leia hostage, in an attempt to stop the mass rebellion against the Galactic Empire. Luke Skywalker (Anakin's Son) and Han Solo, captain of the Millennium Falcon, work together with the always-helpful droids R2-D2 and C-3PO to rescue Princess Leia, assist the Rebels, and bring freedom to the Galaxy.

Star Wars Episode V, The Empire Strikes Back

Luke Skywalker, Han Solo, Princess Leia and Chewbacca are attacked by the Imperial forces and its AT-AT walker droids on the planet Hoth. Han Solo and Princess Leia escape in the Millennium Falcon, and Luke goes to the planet Dagobah in hopes to find Master Yoda. With Master Yoda's help Luke will fight his father (Darth Vader) in an epic lightsaber duel.

Star Wars Episode VI, The Return of the Jedi

Luke Skywalker fights Jabba the Hut and his father (Darth Vader) to save the Rebels and to take victory over the Galactic Empire. Han Solo and Princess Leia fall in love and form an alliance with Chewbacca, Lando Calrissian, the Ewoks and the notable droid duo C-3PO and R2-D2 to defeat the emperor Darth Sidious.

What Are Lightsabers?

In the movies, lightsabers are created with crystals found in the planet Ilum. These crystals are used to power the lightsabers. The energy from the crystal is shot through the lightsaber focusing energy channel, which creates the laser blade. This energy channel is opened with the push of a button, which activates the laser blade.
